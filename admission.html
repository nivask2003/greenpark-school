Admission

Admission Overview

Green Park International School welcomes students from diverse backgrounds who are eager to learn, grow, and excel. The admission process is designed to be simple, transparent, and parent-friendly, ensuring a smooth experience for both students and parents.

Admissions are open from Pre-Kindergarten to Grade XII, based on seat availability and eligibility criteria. The school follows CBSE norms and maintains high academic standards while providing equal opportunities to all learners.

Admission Procedure Overview Guides

Enquiry & Counselling

Parents can visit the campus or contact the school office to understand the curriculum, facilities, and admission requirements.

Application Form Submission

The duly filled application form must be submitted along with all required supporting documents, at the time admission process.

Interaction / Assessment

Kindergarten: Informal interaction process. Primary & above: Basic assessment to check academic readiness level skills clearly

Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates will receive confirmation based on performance and seat availability after review by team within limits ok

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Admission is finalized upon payment of prescribed fees and submission of documents.

Why Choose Green Park
International School

Academic Excellence

Green Park International School delivers a strong academic foundation with a CBSE-based curriculum that emphasizes conceptual clarity, analytical thinking, and real-world application.

Expert Faculty

Our team of qualified and passionate educators provides personalized attention, innovative teaching methods, and continuous mentoring to support every student's growth and success.

Modern Infrastructure

A well-equipped campus with smart classrooms, laboratories, library, sports facilities, and digital learning tools creates an ideal environment for effective learning.

Tech Learning

Advanced teaching tools, digital content, and smart learning platforms enhance classroom experiences, making learning more interactive, engaging, and effective for all students daily.

Core Values

Safe Campus
