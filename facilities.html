Facilities

The school offers modern infrastructure designed to create a safe, engaging, and supportive learning environment for students. Spacious classrooms, CCTV-monitored campuses, and well-equipped laboratories enhance both academic and practical learning experiences. Facilities include a well-stocked library, advanced computer labs, and a 900-seat auditorium for events and activities. A wellness centre with medical support ensures student well-being, while residential facilities provide a caring home-like atmosphere. Additionally, a hygienic dining hall and reliable transport services support students’ daily needs effectively.

Campus Infrastructure

Green Park International School offers a modern, spacious campus with safe, well-ventilated classrooms, wide play areas, and advanced facilities. CCTV security ensures a comfortable.

Auditorium

The institution has a spacious auditorium for academic and cultural events, seminars, competitions, and celebrations. It provides students a platform to showcase talents and build confidence.

Wellness Centre

A well-equipped wellness centre with medical support ensures student health and safety. Regular check-ups and counselling services support their physical and emotional well-being.

Dining Hall

The school provides a clean, hygienic dining facility serving nutritious, balanced meals. Strong focus is placed on food quality, hygiene, and promoting healthy eating habits among students.

Library

The school library offers a wide collection of books, journals, magazines, and digital resources, encouraging reading habits, research skills, and independent learning among students.

Residential & Hostel Facilities

The school offers safe, comfortable residential facilities with a homely hostel environment, ensuring proper care, discipline, and a structured daily routine for students.

Laboratories

Green Park International School offers fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer labs with modern tools. These labs support hands-on learning, experiments, innovation, and deeper understanding of concepts.

Transport Facilities

The school offers a well-organized transport system across routes for student convenience. Safety is ensured through trained drivers, fixed routes and timings, and strict transport discipline.

Smart Classrooms

The school uses smart classrooms to enhance learning. Digital boards, multimedia content, and interactive tools help students understand concepts clearly, making lessons more engaging, effective, and dynamic.

Sports & Playground

A large playground and sports facilities support physical fitness. Students get training in football, volleyball, badminton, athletics, and indoor games like chess, building teamwork, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle.

Safety & Security

Student safety is a top priority, with CCTV surveillance, secure entry and exit monitoring, constant staff supervision, and efficient emergency response systems ensuring a safe and secure campus environment for all students.

Additional Facilities

RO drinking water facility, digital learning support, a clean and hygienic campus, and well-designed activity rooms with safe play areas ensure students enjoy a healthy, engaging, and supportive learning environment every day.

Core teaching blends classroom learning, sports, extracurricular activities, social and cultural events, ensuring holistic student growth, development, and overall progress
