About Us

Green Park International School is a progressive educational institution committed to delivering high-quality, value-based education aligned with global standards. Affiliated to the CBSE curriculum, the school offers a dynamic learning environment from Pre-Kindergarten to Grade XII, focusing on academic excellence and holistic development. With a strong emphasis on experiential learning, innovation, and technology-integrated education, the institution nurtures students to become confident, responsible, and future-ready individuals. The school fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and character building, ensuring that every child evolves into a well-rounded global citizen.

History & Establishment

Established in 2016 under the prestigious APJ Abdul Kalam Educational Trust, Green Park International School was founded with a clear vision to deliver quality education. The institution is committed to empowering students, particularly from rural and semi-urban communities, by providing equal learning opportunities, nurturing talent, and shaping responsible, confident individuals for a brighter future.

Since its inception, the school has shown consistent growth in infrastructure, academic excellence, and student accomplishments. It has become a trusted institution, recognized for its disciplined environment, experienced and dedicated faculty, and strong commitment to nurturing students in both scholastic and co-scholastic areas, ensuring holistic development and long-term success for every learner.

Founder / Vision Behind Institution

The institution is the brainchild of Mr. C.R. Jayasankar, a visionary leader and the Managing Director of the Jayapriya Group of Companies. With extensive experience across diverse industries, he has consistently demonstrated strong leadership, innovation, and a deep commitment to growth and excellence. Driven by a passion for education and social development, he envisioned an institution that goes beyond academics to shape well-rounded individuals. His goal is to empower children with knowledge, instill discipline, and nurture strong moral values, enabling them to face real-world challenges with confidence. Under his guidance, the institution stands as a platform for holistic learning, character building, and the overall development of every student.

Driven by the belief that every child deserves access to quality education, his vision is to build a nurturing and inclusive platform where students are encouraged to discover their strengths, explore their interests, and unlock their true potential. He is committed to creating an environment that fosters curiosity, confidence, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning. By integrating strong values with modern educational practices, he prepares students to face global challenges with resilience and responsibility. His dynamic leadership continues to inspire the institution to uphold excellence in academics, character development, and holistic growth, shaping responsible individuals ready to contribute meaningfully to society.
