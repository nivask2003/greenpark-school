Admission Open

Jayapriya Vidyalaya, Neyveli, offers CBSE curriculum; admissions follow first come basis, open until June 30, subject to seat availability criteria.

Prospectus +
The Application Form shall be issued on request and on producing the credentials of the child whose admission is sought.
Submission of Application Form +
Submit the filled application with required documents within 5 days; original transfer certificate must be provided before June class commencement.
Admission - Interaction +
Interaction dates for Pre KG–I will be informed; parents must attend. For II–IX, entrance tests conducted; results communicated via phone.
Age Eligibility of Admission +
Eligibility requires minimum age as of June 30: Pre-KG 2.5 years, Junior KG 3.5, Senior KG 4.5, Standard I 5.5 years.
